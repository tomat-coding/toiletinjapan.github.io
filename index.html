🚽 Toilets in Japan

Finding a toilet in Japan is usually easy — until you actually need one. This site is a simple guide to help you find bathrooms quickly, especially in cities like Tokyo and Osaka.

💡 Tip: If you need something fast, skip the searching — using a home screen widget that shows the nearest toilet instantly can save time.

📍 Where to find toilets

  • Convenience stores (コンビニ)
  • Train and метро stations
  • Shopping malls and department stores
  • Parks and public facilities

⚠️ Things to know

  • Some toilets close at night
  • Not all are free or easy to access
  • Public toilet data can be inconsistent
